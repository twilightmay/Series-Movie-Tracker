Series & Movie Tracker

Complete Guide & Tutorials

What is Series & Movie Tracker?

The Series & Movie Tracker is a comprehensive web application designed to help you organize, track, and manage all your favorite series and movies in one place. Whether you're binge-watching the latest Netflix series or catching up on classic movies, this tracker gives you complete control over your viewing experience.

With support for multiple user profiles, detailed statistics, progress tracking, ratings, and much more, this tool transforms how you manage your entertainment library. Everything is stored locally in your browser, giving you complete privacy and control over your data.

Key Features

📺 Multi-Content Support

Track both TV series and movies with separate fields and metadata for each type of content.

👤 Multiple User Profiles

Create up to 5 different user profiles, perfect for families or personal collections.

📊 Detailed Statistics

View comprehensive stats including total time watched, completion rates, and genre breakdowns.

⭐ Rating System

Rate your content with a 5-star system and compare with IMDB ratings.

🏷️ Tags & Notes

Organize with custom tags and add personal notes or comments to any item.

📥 Export & Import

Backup your data with easy export/import functionality for data safety and sharing.

🎨 Customizable Themes

Choose from dark, light, or create your own custom color theme.

📋 Watchlist Queue

Create a prioritized watchlist with drag-and-drop reordering.

⏱️ Progress Tracking

Track exactly where you left off with season, episode, and time-based progress.

Getting Started

1. Create Your Profile

When you first open the tracker, you'll need to create a user profile. Click on the hamburger menu (☰) in the top right, then navigate to Profiles and click Change User. From there, you can create a new profile with a custom name and profile picture.

2. Add Your First Content

To add a series or movie, click the + Add Content button in the sidebar. You'll be prompted to choose between adding a Series or Movie. Fill in the required information such as:

  • Title and cover image URL
  • Genre and content type
  • Current status (Watching, Want to Watch, or Completed)
  • Progress information (seasons, episodes, time)
  • Streaming service (for series)

3. Organize Your Collection

Use the filter buttons at the top to quickly switch between "Currently Watching", "Want to Watch", and "Completed" items. You can also use the search bar and advanced filters to find specific content quickly.

Tips & Best Practices

Data Backup

Always remember to export your data regularly! Since everything is stored locally in your browser, clearing cookies or browser data will delete your tracker. Use the Export List feature in the sidebar to create backups.

Recommended Folder Structure

When exporting, we recommend organizing your files in the following structure:

  • Movie and Series Tracker/
  • logs/ (for export files)

Auto-Export Feature

Enable auto-export in Settings to automatically backup your data at regular intervals. This ensures you never lose your tracking progress, even if you forget to manually export.

Using Tags

Tags are a powerful way to organize your content. Use tags like "favorite", "rewatch", "must-watch", or genre-based tags to quickly filter and find what you're looking for.
